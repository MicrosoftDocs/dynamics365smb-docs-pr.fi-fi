## <a name="to-correct-a-posted-sales-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="f309c-125">Kirjatun myyntilaskun korjaaminen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f309c-125">To correct a posted sales invoice</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]  
    >   <span data-ttu-id="f309c-128">Jos **Peruutettu**-valintaruutu on valittuna, et voi korjata kirjattua myyntilaskua, koska se on jo korjattu tai peruutettu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f309c-128">If the **Canceled** check box is selected, then you cannot correct the posted sales invoice because it has already been corrected or canceled.</span></span>
