### <a name="effect-on-customers-and-vendors"></a><span data-ttu-id="27bdc-119">Vaikutus asiakkaisiin ja toimittajiin</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27bdc-119">Effect on Customers and Vendors</span></span>
