## <a name="to-create-and-connect-an-incoming-document-record-from-a-purchase-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="e8a07-111">Saapuvan asiakirjatietueen luominen ja yhdistäminen ostolaskusta</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e8a07-111">To create and connect an incoming document record from a purchase invoice</span></span>
